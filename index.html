How it works

From Post to Hire in Hours,
Not Months

An end-to-end AI pipeline that does the heavy lifting so recruiters only interview the top 10%.

1

Post your requirement

Describe your role in plain English: Skills, CGPA, domain, batch year. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

AI scans every verified profile

Our embedding model analyses 50,000+ profiles using skill graphs — not just keywords — and generates a fit score.

3

Auto-assessment & AI interview

Top candidates get a customised skill test and a voice-based AI interview, summarised for you instantly.

4

Ranked shortlist lands in your inbox

Receive a ranked PDF report of the top 10–25 candidates with scores, interview transcripts and recommendations.
